Request Account Deletion

At Dimandy, we respect your privacy and your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, you may do so using the methods below.

Method 1: Delete via App (Recommended)

You can instantly delete your account directly from the mobile application:

  1. Open the Dimandy App.
  2. Go to Profile / Account tab.
  3. Scroll down and tap on Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your action. Your account will be scheduled for deletion.

Method 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by contacting our support team:

Email: support@dimandy.in

Subject: Account Deletion Request - [Your Registered Phone Number]

Please send the email from your registered email address (if any) or mention your registered phone number clearly. Our team will verify your identity and process the deletion within 30 days.

What Happens to My Data?

When your account is deleted:

  • Your profile information (Name, Phone, Email) is permanently removed.
  • Your saved addresses and payment methods are deleted.
  • Your order history is anonymized for accounting and legal purposes.
