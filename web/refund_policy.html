Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: January 26, 2026

Return Window

You can return most items within 7 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Eligible Items

Items must be:

  • Unused and in original condition
  • In original packaging with tags
  • Accompanied by invoice/receipt
  • Not damaged or altered

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Perishable goods (food, beverages)
  • Personal care items
  • Intimate apparel
  • Customized or personalized items
  • Gift cards

Return Process

  1. Contact customer support within 7 days.
  2. Provide order details and reason for return.
  3. Pack item securely in original packaging.
  4. Schedule pickup or drop-off.
  5. Refund processed within 7-10 business days after receipt.

Refund Method

Refunds will be credited to the original payment method. Processing time varies by bank/payment provider.

Exchange

If you want to exchange an item, return the original item and place a new order for the desired product.
